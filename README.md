A comprehensive technical analysis suite designed for Inner Circle Trader (ICT) and Smart Money Concepts (SMC) practitioners. 
This tool streamlines your workflow by projecting high-probability ranges and institutional price levels across multiple timeframes.

--Key Features--

1. AWR (Average Weekly Range)

Purpose: Visualizes the expected weekly expansion to identify "Draw on Liquidity."

Best Used On: 4H Timeframe.

Benefit: Helps traders anticipate where the weekly candle might wick or close.

2. ADR (Average Daily/Session Range)

Purpose: Projects the potential intraday volatility for the London and New York sessions.

Best Used On: 1H or 15M Timeframes.

Benefit: Prevents over-extending trades when the daily range is already saturated.

3. Standard Deviations (SD)

Purpose: Advanced range projections based on institutional benchmarks.

Included: CBDR (Central Bank Dealers Range), Asian Range SDs, and Flout (Float) levels.

Benefit: Precisely identifies algorithmic take-profit and reversal zones.

4. PO3 (Power of 3)

Purpose: Automatically highlights the Accumulation, Manipulation, and Distribution phases.

Visuals: Plots the Opening Price of your selected timeframe to clearly see the "Judas Swing" below/above the open.

5. HTF (Higher Timeframe Analysis)

Purpose: Overlays HTF structures (PD Arrays, Market Structure) onto your current chart.

Benefit: Maintain "Top-Down" perspective without constant tab-switching, ensuring you never trade against the HTF trend.

--How to Use--

Macro View: Use AWR and HTF on the 4H chart to establish your weekly bias.

Intraday Setup: Drop to the 15M chart to monitor the PO3 and ADR levels.

Execution: Use Standard Deviations to find precise entries and exits within the session ranges.
